Berries Salad

Description

This is my Summer Berry Salad - fresh mixed berries with a light dressing of honey, orange and mint. The perfect quick & easy dinner party dessert!

Ingredients

  • Blueberries
  • Raspberries
  • Strawberries
  • Honey
  • Mint

Steps

  1. Slice mint into thin pieces
  2. Throw berries in bowl then put mint pieces on top
  3. Add some honey
