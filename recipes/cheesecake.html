Cheesecake

Description

With this recipe, a creamy, gorgeous, crack-free New York-style cheesecake is totally doable, even for beginners!

Ingredients

  • Graham Cracker Crumbs
  • Raspberries
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Lemon
  • Cream cheese
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Make graham cracker crust
  3. Stir until combined then press into an even layer
  4. Bake for 10 minutes and reduce temp to 325 degrees
  5. Make cheesecake batter
  6. Bake then let it cool
