Fruit Smoothie

Description

A fresh and tasty easy Fruit Smoothie that takes minutes to make. It is filled with kiwi, banana, blueberries, strawberries and more!!

Ingredients

  • Kiwi
  • Banana
  • Blueberries
  • Strawberries
  • Yogurt
  • Pineapple Juice or Orange Juice
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Pour liquid of choice in blender
  2. Throw in the rest of the ingredients
  3. Blend until it's the consistency you prefer
